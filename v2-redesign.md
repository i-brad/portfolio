# Destiny Braimah — Portfolio Website PRD

> **Version:** 2.0
> **Type:** Personal Portfolio Website
> **Inspiration:** https://www.brimble.io/
> **Goal:** Build a timeless, editorial-style portfolio that tells the story of who I am, what I build, and how I think.

---

# Vision

This is not a portfolio designed to get freelance clients.

This is not a portfolio designed to list technologies.

This is not an online resume.

The website should feel like reading a beautifully designed magazine about a product engineer.

The primary goal is to communicate:

- I solve problems.
- I build products.
- I think beyond code.
- I care deeply about design and user experience.
- I enjoy creating businesses as much as software.
- I ship.

Every page should reinforce this message.

---

# Design Philosophy

The overall experience should feel:

- Minimal
- Elegant
- Editorial
- Confident
- Calm
- Intentional

Avoid:

- flashy animations
- neon gradients everywhere
- glassmorphism overload
- unnecessary motion
- loud colors
- "developer portfolio" clichés

The website should feel expensive.

# Final Guiding Principle

Every design decision should answer one question:

> **Does this make the visitor feel like they're exploring the work of someone who builds thoughtful products?**

If the answer is no, simplify it.

The portfolio should leave people with one lasting impression:

> **"This is someone I'd trust to build and lead exceptional software products."**
